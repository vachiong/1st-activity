Living without a pastime is similar to eating without salt. It is a hobby that adds interest to daily life. It is a hobby or endeavor unrelated to one's job. A hobby brings happiness and enjoyment, one never gets bored with it. It is a hobby, means of unwinding, and recreation. One is using his time wisely while learning something new in this way.

My hobbies are eating, dancing, singing even though I am not a good singer. I've always having chika with those amazing people and to those trusted person. I love watching movies, motocross riding videos and vlogs especially Geo Ong's vlogs. One of my other interests is travel. Every time I visit a new location, I bring my phone and capture intriguing photos of the mountains, the houses, and so on. I love capturing moon and stars. I get tremendous joy and pleasure doing it. I adore these pastimes despite the expense.
