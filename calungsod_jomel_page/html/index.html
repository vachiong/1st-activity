Getting to know more about me

Good day, my name is Jomel Calungsod, and I am from Boljoon, Cebu, but currently residing at Nasipit, Talamban Cebu. Currently taking up, Certificate in Computer Technology in University of San Carlos - Talamban Campus.

My interests before were drafting and architecture, but as time went by, I found myself interested in coding and web development as well, making these two disciplines the perfect choice for me.

I am a bit of a bookworm, especially when I have nothing to do. I love curling up with a good mystery when I am at home! You can see that I don't spend a lot of time outside. I adore rainy days because I can cuddle up with a blanket and eat lots of foods which really gives me satisfaction! I also love coding, doing some drafting and solving mysteries, you know why? It is because I feel like I have a superpower because of it!
