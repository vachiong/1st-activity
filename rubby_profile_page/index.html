I'm Rubby, 21 years of age. I came all the way from Eastern Samar and currently staying here in Cebu City to pursue my dreams. I'm a scholar of Passerelles Numeriques and a 2nd Year student in Computer Technology.

I am an introverted person. I don't speak always but I'm good at listening. One of my strengths is being a fast learner. Since I'm not good at memorizing, I have made an effort to become a critical thinker and be good at analyzation. I have dreamed to become an Accountant before but as what they say, people change. When opporunity to become an IT professional came to me, I did not hesitate to grab it. So now, I am doing my best to acquire the best skills that I can get to become an IT Professional in the future. Through my journey, I have learned different programming languages like Javascript, Java, Python, SQL and also markup languages which are HTML and CSS.

When you don't see me coding, you can find me in my room reading fiction books with a cup of black coffee on the side.
